### `uniform vec3 relativeEyePosition;`

This value stores the world space offset from the player model's head position to the camera's position(ie [`cameraPosition`](/reference/uniforms/general/cameraposition)`-`[`eyePosition`](/reference/uniforms/general/eyeposition)).
