<Aside type="danger">This uniform only works on `1.17` and newer.</Aside>

### `uniform mat3 normalMatrix;`

Equivalent to `mat3(transpose(modelViewMatrixInverse))`, or `gl_NormalMatrix` in older GLSL versions.
