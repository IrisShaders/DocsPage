### `uniform mat4 gbufferModelView;`

Transforms coordinates from player space to view/eye space.
```glsl
vec3 view_pos = gbufferModelView * vec4(feet_player_pos, 1.0);
vec3 view_pos = mat3(gbufferModelView) * eye_player_pos;
```
