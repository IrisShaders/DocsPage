### `sky=<true|false>`

#### Location: shaders.properties

Enables or disables rendering of the vanilla sky in [`gbuffers_skybasic`](/reference/programs/gbuffers/). This includes the sky disc, horizon, and void, however it does not include [`stars`](/reference/shadersproperties/stars)
